About Me

My name is Fakhreddine Hariz, I'm 29 years old from Tunisia, married and I live in Russia since 2019.

I have bachelor degree in IT since 2013, but the situation of our country in that time (after revolution) was hard and finding job it something practically impossible especially IT in that time wasn't so much popular and there was really few companies who recruit only senior developer. So I started to look for different jobs and getting far from web developer career .

I always wanted to be a developer, but even not working in the field, I kept tracking news and programming evolution to be updated. I tried a lot of times to come back and restart my career as web developer but I failed.

Now I'm much more convinced that my ideal job is to be a developer and I should success, I have no choice because i need to support my family especially in this hard situation what hole world passing and it's hard to find job now in Russia especially for a foreigner. So, I started digging on the internet about free sources for learning Tutorials and I found Freecodecamp, w3school, some videos in YouTube which after will lead me to your website.

After days and days of learning and practicing, I started to make my own little projects, and I updated my resume as junior Front-end developer and started to look for Front-end jobs. But nothing happened , and companies are not interested in my little resume. I felt like there is something missing me to start my career and I came with conclusion that I need a guide, a mentor who will show me the start line. I understood that Founders and Coders will be the best guide for me.

Finally, I want really to thank you for your huge job, you guys are really heroes. Helping people to restart from the zero and to give them a chance to live again I can describe only by miracle. I hope that one day also I could help people just like you. Thank you for taking time to read my application and sorry for making a long story about myself.
